Installation Instructions

Insert each caster wheel into the base leg socket until you hear it click into place.
Slide the seat mechanism into the underside of the seat cushion until it is fully seated and locked in position.
Install the backrest on the unit and secure it with four screws until the backrest is tight.
Install the armrests on the unit and secure it with four screws until the armrests is tight.
Install the gas lift cylinder into the center hole of the chair base.
Install the seat assembly onto the gas cylinder and make sure it is fully seated on the top of the cylinder.
